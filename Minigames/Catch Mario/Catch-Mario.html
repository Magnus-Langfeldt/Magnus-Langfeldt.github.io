Catch Mario

In the bustling city of Mushroomopolis, chaos reigns as the notorious thief Mario is on the loose! Known for his lightning-fast reflexes and cunning tactics, Mario has been evading capture, leaving a trail of stolen treasures in his wake.

It's your duty to apprehend the elusive thief Mario and bring him to justice! Equipped with only your mouse and fingers and keen observation skills, you must scour the city streets for any sign of Mario.

But beware! Mario is quick on his feet and adept at blending into his surroundings. He has planted bombs in some of the holes, so be careful!.

Keep your eyes peeled and your reflexes sharp, for every successful capture of Mario brings you closer to restoring peace and order to Mushroomopolis. However, should Mario slip through your grasp, the chase continues.

Will you be able to outsmart the master thief Mario and claim victory for law and order? Test your detective skills, track down the elusive culprit, and become the hero of Mushroomopolis in "Catch the Thief Mario!"

Use your mouse or fingers to catch Mario! Click on him to get points, but if you hit a bomb, you lose!
